The brand's core content unit — pairs a myth with a calm, sourced fact. Orders first, then values; never shames.

```jsx
<MythFact
  myth="Size defines masculinity."
  fact="Confidence is built in the mind, not measured."
  source="Journal of Sex Research, 2015"
  layout="row"
/>
```

`layout`: `stacked` (default, myth above fact) or `row` (side by side). Always include a `source` when a fact references data.
