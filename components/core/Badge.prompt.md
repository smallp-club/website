Small obround label for the Myth → Fact system and quiet category/status markers.

```jsx
<Badge variant="myth">Myth</Badge>
<Badge variant="fact">Fact</Badge>
<Badge variant="neutral">Education</Badge>
```

Variants: `myth` (sienna wash), `fact` (turquoise wash), `neutral`, `accent`, `deep`, `outline`. Sizes `sm | md | lg`. Always pill-shaped.
